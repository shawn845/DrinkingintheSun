How it works

The app uses calibrated pub sun spots and current weather conditions to help you find the best place to sit. Check what is sunny now, see what opens up later, and tap into a pub for directions and the exact sun window.

See the app in action

List view

Browse pubs quickly, see what is sunny now, and check when the next sun window starts.

Map view

See pub locations across Nottingham and use clustered markers to explore the city at a glance.

Pub view

Open a pub to get directions, the best seating location, and the exact sun window for that spot.
