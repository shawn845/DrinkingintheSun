How to use it

  1. Stand at the seating position and hold the phone in portrait.
  2. Tap Enable camera & sensors.
  3. Drag the yellow sun marker onto the real sun, or use the nudge buttons.
  4. Tap Lock alignment.
  5. Switch to Trace skyline and tap along the obstruction edge.
  6. Save the draft to download a JSON file and PNG overlay image.
